📌 A note on what these numbers don't capture

These figures show the financial return on your home as a pure investment — but homeownership is much more than that. A home is a forced savings account that builds equity with every mortgage payment. It's a fixed cost in a world of rising rents. It's roots, stability, and a foundation for your family. For many households, the intangible value of owning — the ability to renovate, to plant a garden, to raise children in a place you can call yours — is worth more than any spreadsheet can show. Use this calculator to go in with clear eyes, not to talk yourself out of a decision that's right for your life.
